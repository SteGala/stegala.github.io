Biography

Hello, I’m Stefano. I am a Research Assistant (Assegnista di Ricerca) and Contract Professor at the Department of Control and Computer Engineering (DAUIN), Politecnico di Torino.

As a member of the NETGROUP (Computer Networks Group), my work primarily focuses on the orchestration of computational resources across the edge-to-cloud continuum, exploring how network software engineering and distributed systems intersect to create more efficient and dynamic infrastructures. My research interests lie in the areas of orchestration for edge-to-cloud computing resources with a focus also on energy consumption.
